Meet our Athletes

Masters of the Sky

Kai Nakamura

Kai Nakamura

Kai Nakamura is a renowned skydiver from Japan, known for pushing the boundaries of human flight. With over 5,000 jumps under his belt, Kai has become one of Asia’s most accomplished athletes in the extreme sports world. His precision and calm demeanor during freefalls have earned him respect and admiration across the skydiving community. Kai's journey began when he was only 18, after seeing a skydiving demonstration in Okinawa that changed his life. Since then, he has pursued his passion with relentless dedication, becoming a certified instructor and competitor.

Luna Estrella

Luna Estrella, known as “The Queen of the Skies”, is a young skydiver whose grace and bravery in the air have captivated audiences worldwide. With over 1,500 jumps from various heights, including extreme jumps from mountains and airplanes over 30,000 feet, Luna’s unique spirit and precision have won her international acclaim. She excels in both team and s olo formations, performing intricate maneuvers with flawless technique and boundless energy. She inspires young people to embrace every jump as an opportunity to see the world from a unique perspective.

Luna Estrella

Conquerors of the Heights

Daniel Rocafuerte

Daniel Rocafuerte

Daniel Rocafuerte, called “The Guardian of the Peaks”, is an experienced climber known for his skill in free climbing and his mastery of challenging ascents. With a focus on refining each technique and a profound respect for nature, Daniel has tackled some of the most demanding routes across North America and Europe, all while promoting environmental preservation. His rigorous training has made him a role model for young climbers. His talent and dedication have earned him recognition in extreme climbing competitions, with a commitment to seeing nature as the ultimate stage for testing human limits.

Maia Cordillera

Maia Cordillera, known as “The Panther of the Rock”, is a fearless and resilient climber who has conquered some of the world’s most challenging peaks and cliffs. Her strength, endurance, and technical expertise have led her to the top in international climbing competitions and expeditions, always searching for new routes and greater challenges. With over 15 years of experience, Maia has completed ascents in the Andes, the Alps, and the Himalayas. Maia organizes workshops for young female climbers and has founded a support organization for women in extreme sports.

Maia Cordillera

Explorers of the Deep

Coral del Mar

Coral del Mar

Coral del Mar, known in the underwater world as “The Mermaid of the Abyss”, is an expert diver who has explored the world's oceans with unparalleled passion. Specializing in deep-sea diving and underwater caves, Coral has braved the depths of reefs, caverns, and open waters with her impressive ability to navigate extreme environments. Her breath-hold skills and serenity below the surface have enabled her to capture stunning images of marine life and participate in ocean conservation research. Coral leads diving workshops for young people, encouraging them to discover the beauty of the oceans and the importance of their preservation.

Leo Tridente

Leo Tridente, nicknamed “The Ocean Warrior”, is a professional diver and underwater photographer who has explored the depths of remote oceans. His talent and courage have led him to uncover submerged treasures and document endangered marine species. Leo combines technical diving with precise photography, capturing the essence of underwater life. Known for his expeditions in protected areas and historic wrecks, Leo has become a true adventurer of the deep. Beyond exploration, Leo uses his imagery to educate others about marine biodiversity and ocean conservation.

Leo Tridente

Eagle’s Wings Award

Eagle’s Wings Award

This prestigious award celebrates exceptional achievements in skydiving, honoring those who soar to new heights with courage and mastery. This year, it was awarded to Luna Estrella for her groundbreaking performances and her commitment to environmental awareness through skydiving. Luna’s passion for both sky and earth has inspired a new generation of skydivers to protect and appreciate the natural world from above.

The Eagle’s Wings Award stands as a testament to the exceptional athletes who continue to redefine the art of skydiving. By earning this honor, Luna Estrella has proven her dedication to both the sport and to environmental awareness, embracing every jump as a chance to inspire. Her performances remind us that courage and conservation can work hand in hand, with every flight leaving a lasting impact on future generations.
